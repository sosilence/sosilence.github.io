Commenting via Twitter

If you have a Twitter account and wish to reply to this post, just tweet what you'd like to say, and make sure to put #mot_{{ page.short }} somewhere in your tweet.

The gobbledygook beginning with '#' is called a 'hashtag', and makes it easy for this site to search Twitter for your tweet, and display it on the correct page.

Alternatively, you can tweet about this post and include its short URL, http://mottr.am/{{ page.short }}, and your tweet will be displayed automatically, whether you use the hashtag or not.
