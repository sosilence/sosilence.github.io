About

Mottr.am is Jack Mottram's weblog.

It's a tumblelog- or microblog-type affair, mostly featuring links to interesting places around the web, images, videos, quotes, with the occasional 'traditional' weblog post.

Colophon

The site is marked up in probably valid HTML5, styled with currently invalid CSS 3, and powered by the excellent static site generator Jekyll, jekyll_ext, and rsync.

The site looks its best in Safari and Google Chrome, followed closely by recent versions of Firefox and Opera; and—much to my surprise!—it looks just fine in Internet Explorer 6-8, and is only lightly mangled by IE5.5. Some pages will fail to render in Firefox 2 and Camino 1.6. Sorry about that.
